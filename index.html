FAQ

Is a tool for studios and players?

-

Both! either if you're a studio or a game player. Alive is so easy a player with no skills can create a 3D assets just from a prompt and a simple editor. But the results are so good a game studio or game developer can use it for their video games.

As a studio, how do I put the asset in the game?

-

Clivi lets you export your generated assets in a variety of formats, including FBX, GLTF, USDZ, and more. You can then import them into your game engine of choice, such as Unity, Unreal Engine, or Godot.

As a player, how do I put the asset inthe game?

-

Clivi lets you export your generated assets in a variety of formats,including FBX, GLTF, USDZ, and more supported by games such us Roblox, Minecraft, CS-GO, Fortnite, and more.
